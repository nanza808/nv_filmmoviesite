About This Site

moviefilmsite is a small, clean project that highlights three movies I love. The goal is to practice clear structure, consistent navigation, and readable design.

Each movie page includes an overview, an image, and a list so the site feels useful and easy to scan.

Why These Three Movies?

Singin' in the Rain

This classic musical is full of energy and unforgettable choreography. It always feels joyful and timeless.

The Bodyguard

I love the mix of romance and suspense, plus the powerful music that makes the story even more emotional.

Mississippi Masala

This film tells a heartfelt story about love and identity, and it highlights the beauty of cultural connection.
